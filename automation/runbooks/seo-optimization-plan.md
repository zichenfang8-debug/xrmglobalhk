# SEO Optimization Plan for xrmglobalhk.com

## Goal

Improve organic visibility for international buyers searching for China procurement, hotel FF&E/OS&E sourcing, AI infrastructure supply, and Southeast Asia project procurement support.

## Primary SEO Positioning

Brand category:

```text
China Procurement Partner
```

Core buyer intents:

- Find reliable China suppliers.
- Source hotel FF&E and OS&E.
- Compare suppliers and quotations.
- Coordinate export-ready procurement.
- Source supporting products for AI/data center infrastructure.
- Manage Thailand and Southeast Asia procurement projects.

## Target Topic Clusters

### China Procurement Partner

- China procurement partner for overseas buyers
- China sourcing agent vs procurement partner
- China supplier verification
- China RFQ support
- China export coordination

### Hotel FF&E / OS&E

- Hotel FF&E procurement from China
- Hotel OS&E supplier sourcing
- Resort furniture procurement China
- Hotel lighting, sanitaryware, casegoods, loose furniture sourcing
- Thailand hotel project procurement

### AI Infrastructure Supply

- AI data center supporting products from China
- UPS suppliers China
- PDU suppliers China
- Server rack suppliers China
- Data center cooling components China
- Cable tray and industrial cable suppliers China

### Cross-Border Trade

- Cross-border procurement China to Southeast Asia
- Export documentation for China procurement
- Supplier quality control before shipment
- Procurement coordination for overseas contractors

## Page-Level SEO Requirements

Every public-facing page draft must include:

- SEO title under 60 characters where possible.
- Meta description between 120 and 160 characters where possible.
- One clear H1.
- Descriptive H2 sections.
- Internal links to relevant service/category pages.
- Buyer-focused CTA.
- No confidential project/client/supplier information unless approved.

## Draft Review Checklist

- Does the page target one search intent?
- Is the H1 literal and buyer-facing?
- Does the meta description explain the commercial offer?
- Are claims specific but not unverifiable?
- Are confidential names redacted?
- Is the CTA RFQ-oriented?
- Are old pages preserved?

## Draft-Only Publishing Rule

SEO workflows may generate content under:

```text
content/drafts/
content/pending-review/
03_CONTENT/_drafts/
```

Publishing requires manual approval and a human commit message.
