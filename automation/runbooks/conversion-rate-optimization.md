# Website Conversion Rate Optimization Plan

## Goal

Increase qualified RFQ and sourcing inquiries from overseas buyers without making unsupported claims or exposing confidential project information.

## Conversion Priorities

1. Make the first-screen positioning clear: China Procurement Partner.
2. Add RFQ-oriented CTAs.
3. Clarify service categories.
4. Show process credibility.
5. Reduce vague marketing language.
6. Add trust signals without naming confidential clients.

## Recommended Page Blocks

- Clear offer headline.
- Buyer problem section.
- Product/service categories.
- How XRM supports buyers.
- RFQ process.
- Quality control and export coordination.
- FAQ.
- Contact/RFQ CTA.

## CTA Examples

- Request RFQ Support
- Send a Product List
- Compare China Suppliers
- Prepare a Supplier Shortlist

## Review Checklist

- Is the page buyer-focused?
- Is the CTA visible without being aggressive?
- Does copy explain what XRM actually does?
- Is confidential information removed?
- Are existing pages preserved?
- Is the page draft-only until approved?
