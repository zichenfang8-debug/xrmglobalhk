# AI Infrastructure Supply from China

XRM helps overseas developers, contractors, data center builders and infrastructure buyers source reliable AI infrastructure supporting products from China.

We focus on the non-chip, project-critical supply chain around AI and data center construction: power, racks, cabling, cooling, fire protection, access control, metal fabrication and export coordination. Every draft RFQ package, supplier shortlist and website update remains pending review until approved.

## The Problem

AI infrastructure projects move fast, but supporting products can become bottlenecks. Buyers often need to compare Chinese suppliers across multiple categories while managing technical specifications, quality risk, export documentation and delivery schedules.

Common problems include:

- Supplier claims that are difficult to verify from overseas
- Incomplete UPS, PDU, rack and cable specifications
- Cooling requirements that change as compute density increases
- Fire protection and access control products that must match local codes
- Fragmented RFQs across electrical, mechanical and low-current packages
- Export coordination gaps between factory, freight forwarder and site team

## Product Categories

### Data Center Supporting Products

We help buyers organize RFQs for supporting infrastructure around server rooms, AI training facilities, edge data centers and modular data center projects.

### UPS and Critical Power

China has a mature supplier base for UPS systems, power modules, battery systems and critical power support products. XRM helps compare brand, topology, redundancy, runtime, maintenance model and after-sales coverage.

### PDU and Rack Power

Rack-level power distribution needs careful attention to socket type, metering, monitoring, branch protection, cable entry, certification and local electrical standards.

### Server Racks and Cabinets

We support RFQs for server racks, network cabinets, containment accessories, cable managers, cabinet PDUs and customized sheet-metal requirements.

### Industrial Cables and Cable Trays

AI infrastructure projects require power cable, fiber cable, control cable, flame-retardant cable, ladder tray, perforated tray, trunking and installation accessories. XRM helps align cable ratings with the destination market.

### Power Distribution Cabinets

We coordinate sourcing for main distribution boards, sub-distribution boards, final distribution boxes, low-voltage switchgear, busduct and related panel components.

### Cooling and Liquid Cooling Components

High-density AI workloads increase demand for precision cooling, rear-door heat exchangers, CDUs, pumps, valves, heat exchangers, sensors and liquid cooling components.

### Fire Protection

We help buyers identify fire alarm, detection, notification and integration products for review by the project fire consultant or local authority.

### Access Control and Security

Access control, video security, perimeter protection and monitoring systems must be reviewed for cybersecurity, privacy and sanctions compliance before procurement.

### Metal Fabrication

Custom brackets, frames, cabinet parts, cable supports and non-critical fabricated parts can often be sourced competitively from China when drawings, tolerances and finish requirements are clear.

## Why Source From China

China offers a deep manufacturing base across electrical, mechanical, metal and low-current systems. For overseas buyers, the advantage is not only unit cost. It is the ability to compare multiple categories, build complete RFQ packages and coordinate production timelines across suppliers.

The opportunity is strongest when the buyer has:

- Clear technical specifications
- A structured RFQ process
- Defined inspection criteria
- Approved packaging and export requirements
- A review process before supplier award
