## Next Verification Checklist

- Request company business license and export entity.
- Request product catalog and latest datasheets.
- Request certificates by exact SKU, not only brand-level certificates.
- Confirm target-country standards: IEC/UL/CE/CPR/NFPA/local fire code as applicable.
- Confirm minimum order quantity, lead time, Incoterms, warranty and overseas service process.
- Screen sanctions, cybersecurity and end-user restrictions before including security products in RFQ.
